DJ Lycan


My primary objective in professional life is to represent my nation in the international clubbing scene. I play & produce techno music. I started learning the fine art of DJ'ing at the age of 19. Music has always been in my veins. Even before coming to the DJ'ing scene I learned Indian heavy classical music at Bhartiya Kala Kendra , New Delhi.

Now I have ambitions to showcase the Indian EDM talent at an international platform.

About Me

Lycan (Dj Lucky) Born in Mhow brought up in Delhi and shifted to Indore a city known for its musical background and history, this DJ has made ripples and created a niche for himself. His interest in music started when he was 4 years old , he has dabbled in all kinds of music like pure Indian classical vocals , light classical vocals as well as keyboard and roto drums. He enrolled in bhartiya kala kendra , asias biggest and most renowned classical music academy. He was awarded as best child keyboard artist at Bhartiya Kala Kendra during his school days. The prodigal kid shifted to Indore in 2004. His resolve to become a DJ intensified and he started to learn the tips of the trade , after finishing his formal training in the fine art of Dj ing , he debuted as resident Dj at Geoffrey Pub , Indore , and his music struck a platonic chord with the audience. The journey had begun and soon Lycan found himself grooving at the most happening joint of the city .... Critical Mass ! , later on becoming resident DJ at Quorum , Sayaji Hotels ,Indore.

His passion to perform and serve something new opened him to play with many international artists like Jerome Isma AE , Funkagenda , Mat Zo , Albin Myers, Moti , Alvero ,Futuristic Polar Bears ,Arjun Vaghale , Nikhil Chinnapa , Suketu , akbar sami ,alvaro,shaan,R3HAB ,HARD ROCK SOFA , MAT Zo , Zaeden and many more
